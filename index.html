Your Mission, Should You Choose To Accept It...

Is to embark on a battle of wits, solving puzzles, cracking codes, and testing your mettle against the metal machine that stands in your way between you and a prize of great value.

You will be faced with many enigmas...some of which you may be able to solve on your own...but mostly not. You must place your utmost confidence in fellow agents; choose your alliances carefully.

A Few Rules...

You will each obtain unique login information "encrypted" in the email that shared this webpage's link. Do not share your login information with anyone, even trusted allies. You may, however, share any content on your unique webpages.

You are welcome to use any resources you can manage...many puzzles may need a quick Google search to learn code-breaking methodologies, perhaps you'll have to reference reading material, or even interview witnesses to obtain important information.

Above all, be flexible, clever, good-humored, and you may find yourself enjoying this adventure. Best of luck, Ryan.
